# 📊 Superstore Sales & Profitability Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes transactional sales data from a retail Superstore to uncover actionable business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional performance, and overall business growth.

Using SQL for data analysis and Power BI for interactive dashboards, this project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow from raw data exploration to executive-level business recommendations.

Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day.

Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to answer critical business questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which regions perform the best?
- Which customers contribute the most revenue?
- Are discounts reducing profitability?
- Which markets require immediate attention?

This project answers these questions using SQL and presents the results through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Evaluate product profitability.
- Identify high-value customers.
- Perform Pareto (80/20) Analysis.
- Conduct RFM Customer Analysis.
- Analyze regional and state performance.
- Evaluate discount impact on profitability.
- Generate executive-level business recommendations.

## Key Business Insights

- Technology products generated the highest overall revenue.
- A small percentage of customers contributed the majority of total sales (Pareto Principle).
- Several products generated high sales but negative profit.
- Moderate discounts maintained profitability, while excessive discounts resulted in losses.
- Certain states generated strong sales but operated at a loss.
- Profitability should be prioritized alongside revenue growth.

## Business Recommendations

- Review discount strategies to improve profit margins.
- Strengthen loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Optimize pricing for loss-making products.
- Investigate underperforming regions.
- Focus on sustainable profit growth rather than revenue alone.
